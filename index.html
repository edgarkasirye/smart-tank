The smart water tank equips tank owners as well as water suppliers a means to keep track of the water consumption

THE SMART TANK

We are often busy and therefore ignore the possibilty of water shortage with in our homes and respective institutions and are only awekened when the water supply is exhausted or the brink of exhaution. Further more we find difficulty in relating what we assume is our water consumption rate toour recieved monthly water bills. Thats where the smart tank comes in.

How smart is the SMART TANK?

The MART TANK is your everyday tank tiped with technological features to keep track of water level within your tank. An ultrasonic sensor is added to measure water level at specified intervals. You dont need to worry about the rude awekening of an empty tank because a notification shall be sent directly to your mobile phone alerting you that the water is at critical level. Easy peasy lemon squeezy!

And the best part is you dont have to worry about installation cause our team's got you covered.

If you ever want regular monitoring and tracking of your water consumption rate, sign up to our easy to use dash board wada-water tank.com.

A solution to suprising high water bills and abrupt water shortage.

Get to know the your water tank levels and quantity when anywhere and at anytime.
